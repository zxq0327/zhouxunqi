About

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the School of Control and Computer Engineering, North China Electric Power University, under the supervision of Professor Yongjie Zhai.

As a primary researcher, I have participated in two General Program projects funded by the Natural Science Foundation of Hebei Province, one Innovation Capacity Development Project for Graduate Students in Hebei Province, and one university–industry collaborative research project. I have published nine papers in leading international journals, including Information Fusion (IF: 17.4), Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence (IF: 9.0), and Advanced Engineering Informatics (IF: 11.5), and have been granted two patents.

My research interests include multimodal pre-training, knowledge graphs, and visual reasoning, with a particular focus on their applications to intelligent vehicle damage assessment.
